# Tens Solitaire - QML Edition

Discard any pair of cards which total ten.
The valid pairs are:
* A and 9
* 2 and 8
* 3 and 7
* 4 and 6
* 5 and 5

Tens and face cards (J, Q, K) may be eliminated too.
